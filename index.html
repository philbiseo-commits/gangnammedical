FAQ

Common questions

Are these clinics ranked from 1 to 10?

No. We use a Top 10 format, but clinics are displayed as selected picks instead of in a strict numbered order.

Is this an official hospital website?

No. This is a discovery-style clinic guide built for international visitors researching surgery in Korea.

How are clinics selected?

We focus on treatment fit, international-patient friendliness, communication clarity, and information quality.

Can a clinic request updates?

Yes. Clinic owners and representatives can contact us to request profile corrections, updates, or partnership conversations.
